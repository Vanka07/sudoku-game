Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Sudoku Minimalist does not collect any personal information. We do not gather:

All game data — including your statistics, game progress, settings, and preferences — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Sudoku Minimalist does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

  • Social media SDKs

Internet Connection

Sudoku Minimalist does not require an internet connection to function. The app works entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data whatsoever, the app is safe for users of all ages. We comply with COPPA and other applicable children's privacy regulations.
