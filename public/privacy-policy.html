Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Sudoku Minimalist ("the App") is developed by Vanka07. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game data (progress, statistics, settings) is stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

All data is stored on your device using local storage. This includes:

  • Game progress and saved games
  • Statistics (games played, best times, streaks)
  • User preferences (theme, sound, haptic settings)
  • Daily challenge progress

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App is suitable for all ages and contains no inappropriate content.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
